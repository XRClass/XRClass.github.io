About XR Class

Making Education more GLOBALLY ACESSIBLE

We notice many people around the world do not have an interest in checking up on their health. Millions of people suffer from preventable illnesses and diseases. We know if we could reach a fraction of these people, we could increase global interest in health education.

increases excitement about health education and provides people globally with a lower barrier to entry in quality health care.

The project is fully owned and operated through the three of us.

is a free software that allows for diagnosing physically detectible diseases such as Color Blindness, Presbycusis, Parkinson's Disease, and more through Virtually and Augmented Reality. Currently the engine is being built through Unity Engine but in the future will be deployed on the Web through WebGL to make it even more accessible.

is being developed as a platform for researching and serving healthcare for communities. Because of our mission, we are investing time into learning more about the needs of patients that use the software and what we can do to make the user experienece and accuracy of our product better.
